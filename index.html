What is 'Flex First, Fix Later'?

This retirement decumulation approach involves a period of flexible drawdown from your investments during the early, more active years of retirement. This provides greater control and potential for growth. Later in retirement, you transition to purchasing an annuity to secure a guaranteed fixed income for your remaining life.
